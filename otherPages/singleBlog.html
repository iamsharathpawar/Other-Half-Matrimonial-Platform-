Now let's take a look at what medical tests should be done before marriage-
blood group
It is very important to know the blood group of the bride and groom. Because if negative and positive blood group boys and girls get married, there will be problems in having children later. Many times the child dies in the womb. Or the baby may get jaundice after birth. Brain damage can also occur.
Sexually Transmitted Tests
It is important to know if there is any sexually transmitted infection in the body of the bride or groom. Only a medical test can tell if the bride or groom has sexually transmitted diseases like HIV, Gonorrhea, Syphilis.
Sperm test
Many times husband and wife have to face difficulties in conceiving children. This may be due to incapacity of the husband or wife. If the male has any disability, it can be detected by sperm test before marriage.
